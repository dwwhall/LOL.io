Intial Design

Rationale

I wanted the map to be the first thing people saw when they entered the website. All additional graphs are below because the scatterplot of deaths and pumps on the Cholera map was the most important. Each following chart is ordered in importance to the findings. There is some value to in the age bar chart because it is important to know that the majority of deaths occur in ages 0-10 and over 80. The gender pie chart does not provide much knowledge.

Bugs in the system

The map is not connected to the timeline. I tried to set up an id for each death point on the map, relating the timeline to each id. The 'id' approach was demonstrated in the data visualization class, but it did not work for my data files. If I were going to try again, then I would try to connect the graphs through date.

Observations

From my visualization, the center pump is located near the most deaths, so this is probably one of the sources of Cholera.

It is interesting to note that the majority of deaths occur in ages 0-10 and over 80.

The gender does not play a factor because it is almost a 50/50 split for deaths of males and females.
